How It Works · ralphex mode
Solvulator follows the ralphex loop: plan-by-stage execution, strict gates, and human review before final output.
01 Parse
Extract structured legal facts, deadlines, and obligations.
02 LATHE
Decompose weak claims and repair reasoning failures.
03 Draft
Generate Hebrew legal draft from repaired claims only.
04 Review Gate
Reviewer approves, rejects, or nudges the next run.
Core loop: run stage → inspect output → nudge → rerun → approve.
